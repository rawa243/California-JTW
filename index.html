Journey to Work (2016):

Commuting trends by California counties

About this map:

  • Created: April 22, 2018
  • Data sources:
    Amerian Community Survey (2016)
    TIGER/Line U.S. Census Bureau (2017)
  • Map authored by: Rebekah Watkins
  • Find me on GitHub: @raw243

This map highlights the historic trends in various commuting methods taken to and from work for the working population in each California county during 2016.
**Other Methods include commuting by bicycle, motorcycle, taxi or any other method.
Continue to the U.S. Census Bureau's website to learn more about Journey to Work data and trends
